About Us


White Buffalo Woman is a young, howling mess that is crouching on all fours and readying itself to pounce on the world of rock ’n’ roll.

The band started in Minerva, Ohio in drummer Joe’s basement, below their family-run funeral home. Members include Evan Rutledge (Vocals), Adam Murphy (Guitar), Alex Leggett (Bass) Devin Bezeredi (Guitar) and Joe Wales (Drums). The band fuses rock, blues, psychedelic, souland punk into an explosion of new music.

They have played across the US and opened for acts like Dex Romweber, Patrick Sweany, The Buffalo Killers, Dan Sartain, Jacuzzi Boys, The Ettes, James Leg and The Stone Foxes.


Their first full-length album, White Buffalo Woman, was released by Grimtale in early 2016. They have released a 7” vinyl record “My Days/ This Town” (released on Grimtale Records) in 2012, followed by a self released four song EP in 2013 and a 7″ single “Like You Used To Do/Always on My Mind” released on Grimtale records in 2014. Their music has earned them airplay on Little Steven’s “Underground Garage” on SiriusXM and a thumbs-up from J. Geils' frontman/singer Peter Wolf. Their musical style ranges from organ-laced garage rock to Southern-fried soul to uncannily Stonesy, all great fun.
